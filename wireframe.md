## Design Notes

### Colors
- **Primary:** Purple (#9333ea)
- **Secondary:** Lime (#84cc16)
- **Neutral:** Gray scale
- **Success:** Green (for checkmarks, badges)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, tight line-height
- **Body:** Regular, relaxed line-height
- **CTAs:** Semibold, uppercase

### Components
- **Buttons:** Rounded corners, hover lift effect
- **Cards:** Subtle shadow, hover elevation
- **Badges:** Rounded pill shape, vibrant colors
- **Inputs:** Clean borders, focus state with purple ring

### Animations
- **Electric pulse:** Subtle glow on CTAs
- **Hover effects:** Lift + shadow on cards
- **Carousel:** Auto-advance every 5s
- **Stats:** Count-up on scroll into view (optional)
